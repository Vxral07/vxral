Company Assets Life Cycle

Acquisition

Assets are acquired for company use.

Deployment

Assets are deployed to designated locations or employees.

Utilization

Assets are actively used in daily operations.

Maintenance

Regular maintenance and servicing to ensure asset functionality.

Upgrade/Repair

Assets are upgraded or repaired as needed to prolong their lifespan.

Disposal

Assets are disposed of when they reach the end of their useful life or are no longer needed.
